Lesson 13: Assessing student learning — Particles matter supervised assessment

Assessment purpose

To assess student understanding in the topics of particle theory, states of matter, the periodic table, elements, compounds and mixtures and assess their understanding of how these topics relate to real world applications and scientific investigations.

Learning Activities:

Introduce the assessment

•     Review with students the separate sections of the assessment and ensure they understand what they are expected to do.

•     Discuss the Guide to making judgments with students to highlight the assessable elements for the assessment, and discuss what responses would look like at each of the standards A–E.

•     Provide students with an opportunity to clarify any components of the exam.

Assessment

•     Have students individually complete the sheet Particles matter supervised assessment to demonstrate their understanding of the topics of particle theory, states of matter, the periodic table, elements, compounds and mixtures, and to demonstrate their understanding of how these topics relate to real world applications and scientific investigations.

•     Collect student responses.

•     Discuss the exam to allow for student reflections.
